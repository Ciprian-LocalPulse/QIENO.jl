# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and evidence-driven research
community. Be constructive, distinguish measurements from hypotheses, credit
prior work, and welcome questions asked in good faith.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate misinformation, and
the publication of another person's private information are unacceptable.

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository owner through their
GitHub profile. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
